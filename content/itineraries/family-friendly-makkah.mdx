Let's be honest: taking kids to Makkah is a different experience. The spirituality is still there, but so are the meltdowns, the "I'm tired", and the negotiating over screen time. This itinerary is designed around that reality.

<Collapsible title="Who's it for">

- Families with children of any age visiting Makkah
- Parents who want a realistic plan that accounts for naps, meals, and energy levels
- Anyone who came back from a family trip feeling like they needed a holiday from the holiday

**The golden rule:** Rested kids = good experience. Tired kids = bad experience for everyone. Every decision in this itinerary flows from that principle.

</Collapsible>
