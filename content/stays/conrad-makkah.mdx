The Conrad represents the new Makkah — modern architecture, contemporary interiors, and amenities that feel current rather than traditional. The Jabal Omar development surrounding it adds a layer of convenience that older hotels can't match.

## The Design

Clean lines, neutral tones, and thoughtful lighting. The rooms feel fresh and uncluttered, which is a welcome contrast to the ornate style of many Makkah hotels. Good workspace if you need to handle emails between prayers.

## Jabal Omar Mall

Direct hotel access to the mall means shopping, dining, and essentials are available without stepping outside. The mall has international brands, local shops, and a solid food court.

## The Walk

Four minutes to the Haram is slightly further than the Clock Tower properties, but the walk is through the well-maintained Jabal Omar district. It's pleasant and safe at all hours.

## Our Take

The Conrad is for travellers who appreciate modern design and don't want their hotel to feel dated. The Jabal Omar location adds genuine lifestyle convenience, and the walk to the Haram is short enough to feel seamless.
